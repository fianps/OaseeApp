Feature

Our app name is derived from the word 'oasis'. Just like an oasis in a desert area, we hope this app can provide refreshment for users amidst the rampant spread of fake news. OASEE can also be pronounced as "ooo I see". We want users to have a better understanding of the news until they say "ooo I see" to express their comprehension.

Fake News Detection

Fake news detection is the process of identifying and distinguishing false or misleading information from genuine and accurate news.

Text Summarization

News Text Summarization is the process of condensing a news article into a concise summary using automated techniques.

Sentiment Analysis

News Sentiment Analysis is the process of determining the emotional tone or sentiment expressed in news articles or headlines.
